What is the Wildy Minecraft Server?

The AxleCraft.com Wildy Minecraft Server is a survival server that was released on January 10, 2022. Members can choose where they want to construct their own bases in the open world PvP wilderness. Only a few sanctuary safe zones are permanent in the world like the Spawn Fortress. In the wild, you start out with nothing and must set up a home base to acquire resources & upgrade your gear. Ally with other members by joining a clan or registering your own, then construct team bases. If you make a legendary base in the wild, AxleCraft may feature it in a video. Members can optionally choose to compete in ranked PvP arena duels or clan capture the flag battles for gear and loot.

How to Join and Play on the Server

Membership costs $40 for server lifetime access, or $10 per month. Paypal & most crypto coins are accepted. To join send an application to AxleCraft#2110 on Discord.

Send an Application to AxleCraft#2110 on Discord with this info:

  • Your email address
  • Your minecraft username
  • Your youtube username
  • Payment type (if crypto, include coin name, amount & wallet address you want to send from)
  • A statement saying youve read and agree to the server rules

Server Rules

In the wildy, the state of the world is up to the members. Admins reserve the right to ban to promote server quality, and to make sure all progress is earned. If you feel your ban was unjust send an appeal

Warning: A ban and loss of membership will occur if you violate any of the the following:

  • No hacks, exploits, duping, or modded clients are allowed. Anything that gives you an advantage is banned such as x-ray vision or auto diamond mining. If you are detected using a modded client or it is obvious that you are and other players report you, you will be banned
  • Dont use obsidian or other similar blocks to obstruct spawn point (0,0), or to trap other players who cant escape from it
  • Dont destroy the marked walled sanctuary zones, main highways or nether highways (+/- x, +/- z)
  • All names, language, and conduct must remain PG-rated since this server may be streamed
  • Admins will never spawn items or cheat in any way. We want the server to be a top notch survival experience where each members effort affects the world

If your application is missing data it will be denied. This application filtering is intended to create a high quality group of members on this server. You can use https://privnote.com/ to send your application data over discord. Please dont send any coins until your application has been approved by AxleCraft unless you just want to donate to help the server, then thank you! If your application is approved, you can choose to send to the wallet address provided.

Most Crypto Coins Accepted

Most liquid coins and chains are supported. When you send in your application AxleCraft will notify you if the coin you want to use is not accepted before you send anything.
